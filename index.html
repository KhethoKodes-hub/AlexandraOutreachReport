Key Findings from Alexandra
  • Severe Water Shedding: 58% experience frequent water shortages, with poor communication about schedules
  • Drainage Crisis: Widespread drainage system failures causing flooding and sanitation issues
  • Pollution Problems: River pollution from littering and illegal dumping creating health hazards
  • Infrastructure Neglect: 83% rate water infrastructure as "Poor" or "Very Poor"
  • High Engagement Potential: Strong interest in workshops and water conservation education

Recommendations & Action Plan

Evidence-based interventions for Alexandra water management

Priority Recommendations for Alexandra
  • Emergency Communication System: Implement SMS alerts and community noticeboards for water shedding schedules
  • Drainage Rehabilitation Program: Immediate repair of blocked and broken drainage systems
  • Community Education Campaign: Water conservation workshops targeting 79% interested residents
  • River Clean-up Initiative: Community-led program to address pollution and littering
  • Leak Reporting App: Simple mobile app for reporting leaks with GPS location
  • Local Plumber Network: Train and deploy local residents for quick leak repairs
