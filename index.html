Key Findings from 85-Survey Dataset
  • Drainage Crisis (62%): 53 out of 85 households report drainage system failures
  • Sewer Blockages (56%): 48 households report sewer overflows as major issue
  • Pollution Problems (53%): 45 households report river and drain pollution
  • Infrastructure Neglect: 65% rate water infrastructure as "Poor" or "Very Poor"
  • High Engagement Potential: 83% interested in workshops, 77% willing to adopt conservation
  • Education Gap: Only 36% received water conservation education
  • Communication Gap: 63% don't know how to report water issues
  • Housing Type: 59% live in brick houses, 24% in informal settlements

Recommendations & Action Plan

Evidence-based interventions for Alexandra water management (85-survey analysis)

Priority Recommendations for Alexandra
  • Drainage Emergency Program: Immediate repair of blocked drainage systems affecting 62% of residents
  • Sewer System Rehabilitation: Address sewer blockages reported by 56% of households
  • Community Education Campaign: Water conservation workshops targeting 83% interested residents
  • Anti-Littering Initiative: Community-led program to address pollution (53% concern)
  • Infrastructure Maintenance Plan: Regular maintenance schedule for water infrastructure
  • Local Maintenance Teams: Train residents for quick response to leaks and blockages
  • Communication System: Regular updates on water shedding and maintenance schedules
  • Reporting Education: Train community on how to report water issues effectively
